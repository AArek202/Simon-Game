Have you ever played Simon? well, in case you did, good for you, go ahead and try this website. If you did not, basically, Simon is an interactive game that tests your memory by requiring you to repeat a sequence of colors and sounds. As the game progresses, the sequence becomes longer and more complex, testing your ability to remember the pattern.

How do I play Simon??

The game starts when Simon flashes a random button (color) along with its sound. The player must press the same button. Simon then adds another button to the sequence. The player repeats the entire sequence in the correct order. The sequence continues to grow, testing the player's memory.
